About Me

I have experience working in startups, taking on different roles, and continuously improving through self-learning from the internet. I graduated with a Bachelors Degree in Computer Science at California State University of Stanislaus. Through the time spent getting my degree, my fascination shifted a bit towards UX and UI design. Once I started self learning more about UX/UI, I knew I would love UX/UI as a career. I enjoy researching, validating assumptions, listening to feedback, understanding why, keeping up to date with design trends, and collaborating with the team.

I am here to evolve as a UX/UI designer, here to create a great experience for people, here to listen to people's feedback and make it even better; here to not let people get frustrated and give up.
