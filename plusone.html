Too Long; Didn't Read (tl;dr)
My goal was to create a new mobile design for PlusOne Social. Many consumers go on the app daily, but the app needed some redesigning, such as redesigning the onboarding and events section. There was also some restructuring and renaming content, such as menu navigation.

Company: Titan AI Studios Inc. creates apps for users. A specific app I was working on by the company was called PlusOne Social. PlusOne Social allows users to meet new friends daily based on their interests. Users may create events, such as a concert event and find users to go with. The app is currently only available in the bay area.

The Problem: We noticed the onboarding and events section of the design was not pleasing to the eye.

    Diving in deeper:
  • when users go to the "events" section, the images of the users are enlarged, therefore, wasting too much space
  • colors of the app needed a brighter color, that resembles friendship and happiness, rather than a dark color that depicts sadness or seriousness

Role: Lead Designer

Tools:

  • Sketch
  • Balsamiq Mockups 3
  • Zeplin



Findings

- Wireframes -
I created 2 visual interface design systems, which I iterated using a wireframed screen-flow for the client to chose from. I was constantly keeping him updated with the wireframes, so any changes made could be changed in the earlier stages. After a few iterations, the client chose one that he liked, which resulted as the final wireframe listed below.






- Mockups -

Create Event Screen: instead of an actual photo, I designed it to a cartoon look and feel that illustrates the bay area

Events Screen: removed the enlarged image that is wasting space to where more information could be included; instead I added user profile image, date, title, and tags to the left of the column, whereas the photo of the event will be to the right

Hamburger Menu Screen: restructured the menu items, such as removing "Close Account" and hiding it in "Other options" and having the "Logout" listed as the last item on the menu

Create Profile Screen: Renamed the heading to "Create Your Profile" instead of "Welcome to PlusOne!", allowing users to know what page they're on; added age filtering to where users can filter the age they would like to meet



- Styles -
I wanted to create very simple, yet "happy" color palette where only one major color will pop out and reach out to the audience. Pink is depicted as playful, sensitive, and safety, which suited the app. I used Avenir font as it is a simple, modern, and easy to read combined with the color.



Key Learnings

PlusOne Social received a new design for the audience, which received good ratings and feedback by conducting surveys and interacting with users.
Limitations. I had to learn to limit some features, thus this helped me understand what was important and what was not important.
