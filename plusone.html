PlusOne Social

The app needed redesigning with different colors and information architecture.



Company: Titan AI Studios Inc. is a company that creates apps, such as PlusOne Social. PlusOne Social allows people to meet new friends every day based on their interests, as well as creating events and finding friends to go with.

The Problem: The screens needed a redesign of color and organization

    Diving in deeper:
  • redesigning mobile app colors that represent a brighter color, that resembles friendship and happiness, rather than a dark color that depicts seriousness
Role:
  • UX/UI Designer Intern
  • Marketing Intern
Programs Used:
  • Sketch
  • Balsamiq Mockups 3
  • Zeplin
  • Pen & Paper
Team:
  • iOS/Android Developer

Findings

- Wireframes -

I created 3 lo-fi wireframes and constantly updating 1 wireframe to the team, so any changes could be modified in the earlier stages.








- Mockups -

  • Create Event Screen: instead of an actual photo, I designed it to a cartoon look and feel that illustrates the bay area

    What I would do differently now:
  • on "Create your own Event", I would organize the options by alphabetical order, thus making it easier for people to find an option
  • remove "Use a PlusOne Event" since that isn't "creating" the event, it's "attending" an event that's already been created; thus, I would move that section to "Events" screen



  • Events Screen: removed the enlarged image to fit important information; instead, I added user profile image, date, title, and tags to the left of the column, whereas the photo of the event will be to the right

    What I would do differently now:
  • on the horizontal scroll of the days, I would highlight which day I am on (e.g. If I am on "Today", there would be an indicator letting me know I'm on "Today")
  • add a filter icon that allows people to filter days, types of events, age, etc.



  • Hamburger Menu Screen: restructured the menu items, such as hiding "Close Account" and having the "Logout" listed as the last item on the menu

    What I would do differently now:
  • removing hamburger menu completely since it's most likely to be "missed" by people, and move all that information to "Profile" screen



  • Create Profile Screen: Renamed the heading to "Create Your Profile" instead of "Welcome to PlusOne!", thus allowing users to know what page they're on; added age filtering to users to specify certain age they would like to meet

    What I would do differently now:
  • remove the bottom navigation, thus allowing people to focus on creating their profile instead of exploring outside the screen
  • their are 2 call-to-action buttons, so change the back button to outline button instead of filled in


Key Learnings

PlusOne Social received good ratings and feedback by conducting surveys and interacting with people. Limitations. I had to learn to limit some features, thus this helped me understand what to prioritize.
