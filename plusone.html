PlusOne Social

App Store PlusOne Social

Google Play Store PlusOne Social

Too Long; Didn't Read (tl;dr)
The app needed some redesigning of onboarding and events section, as well as restructuring and renaming content, such as menu navigation.

Company: Titan AI Studios Inc. is a company that creates apps, such as PlusOne Social for users. PlusOne Social allows users to meet new friends daily based on their interests, as well as creating events and finding friends to go with.

The Problem: We noticed the onboarding and events section of the design was not pleasing to the eye.

    Diving in deeper:
  • when users go to the "events" section, the images of the users are enlarged, therefore, wasting too much space of important information that was needed
  • colors of the app needed a brighter color, that resembles friendship and happiness, rather than a dark color that depicts sadness or seriousness

Role:

Lead Designer



Tools:
  • Sketch
  • Balsamiq Mockups 3
  • Zeplin



Findings

- Wireframes -
I created 2 visual interface design systems, which I iterated using a wireframed screen-flow. I was constantly updating the wireframes to the team, so any changes could be modified in the earlier stages.



- Mockups -

Create Event Screen: instead of an actual photo, I designed it to a cartoon look and feel that illustrates the bay area

Events Screen: removed the enlarged image to fit important information; instead, I added user profile image, date, title, and tags to the left of the column, whereas the photo of the event will be to the right

Hamburger Menu Screen: restructured the menu items, such as hiding "Close Account" and having the "Logout" listed as the last item on the menu

Create Profile Screen: Renamed the heading to "Create Your Profile" instead of "Welcome to PlusOne!", thus allowing users to know what page they're on; added age filtering to users to specify certain age they would like to meet

- Styles -
I wanted to create very simple, yet "happy" color palette where only one major color will pop out and reach out to the audience. Pink is depicted as playful, sensitive, and safety, which suited the app. I used Avenir font as it is a simple, modern, and easy to read combined with the color.



Key Learnings

PlusOne Social received good ratings and feedback by conducting surveys and interacting with users.
Limitations. I had to learn to limit some features, thus this helped me understand what to prioritize.
