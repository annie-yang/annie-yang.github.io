I graduated with a Bachelors of Science in Computer Science from California State University of Stanislaus. I have experience working in startups, taking on responsibilities from different roles and continuously evolving through self-learning from the power of internet. Users need security (purchasing products, valid information, etc.), value (saving the user's time, achieving goals, etc.), and straightforwardness when accessing a product. My purpose is for users to have a good experience by eliminating any difficult tasks and prioritizing relevant information to depict a clear and easy flow for users.

My process begins with understanding business products and goals to acquire a better understanding of the audience and company background. I then take on the approach to create a competitive assessment to understand the competitive market. After, I build information architecture by making sure the user receives valid and structured content across products. I then continue with creating prototypes and getting continuous feedback. My process is a repeating cycle to gather as much feedback as possible and continuing to create an even better prototype than the first one. With all this mind, my job is to also empathize with the user in order to deliver the best products to them.
