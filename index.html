I have experience working in startups, taking on different roles, and continuously improving through self-learning from the internet. When accessing a product, users look out for security (purchasing products, valid information, etc.), value (saving the user's time, achieving goals, etc.), and straightforwardness. My job is to help users eliminate any difficult tasks, present relevant information by priority, and depict a clear and easy flow for users so that they may have a good experience.

My process begins with understanding the business’ products, the target audience, and company background to develop company goals. Secondly, I research about the market by conducting competitive assessment and analysis to understand more about the space. Next, an information architecture is built so that users receive valid and structured content across products. I then continue proceed to test with prototypes for continuous feedback to create better features/product that users need before investing resources into building. Every step of my process empathizes with the user in order to deliver only the best experience.
